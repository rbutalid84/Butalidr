Claveria, is a first class municipality in the province of Misamis Oriental, Philippines. It is also the largest in terms of land area in the province. The people from mindanao called it as a mini Baguio next to Malaybalay City in Bukidnon because of its foggy ambiance with cold air and water and it always rain in Claveria which is good for the crops.
